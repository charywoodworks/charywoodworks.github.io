A king-size bed with full hydraulic lift storage, built for a couple in Miyapur. The bed features an upholstered headboard in grey fabric, walnut veneer finish on all visible surfaces, and a gas-spring hydraulic mechanism that makes accessing under-bed storage effortless. The frame is built on engineered wood with solid wood edge supports for long-term durability.
