MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard) is the preferred material when you want smooth, painted finishes without visible wood grain. Its uniform density makes it perfect for CNC-routed designs, profile edges, and flawless paint application.

We use MDF for TV units, display shelves, kids' furniture, wall paneling, and mandir units where a painted or lacquered finish is desired. Available in standard and moisture-resistant grades for different applications.
