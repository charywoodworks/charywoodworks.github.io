Wood Polymer Composite (WPC) boards are the ideal solution for areas exposed to moisture and humidity. Unlike plywood or MDF, WPC is 100% waterproof, termite-proof, and does not swell or warp in wet conditions.

We use WPC for bathroom vanities, balcony storage, kitchen cabinets near sinks, and outdoor furniture. Available in various thicknesses and finished with PVC laminates or paint for a clean modern look. Slightly heavier than plywood but far more durable in humid conditions.
