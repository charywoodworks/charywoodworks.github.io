Laminates are decorative surface sheets applied over plywood or MDF to provide color, texture, and protection. Available in hundreds of patterns — wood grain, solid colors, stone textures, and abstract designs. Options include matte, gloss, textured, anti-fingerprint, and super-matte finishes. Highly scratch-resistant and easy to clean. Brands like Merino, Greenlam, and Century offer consistent quality. The most cost-effective way to achieve varied looks across furniture pieces.
