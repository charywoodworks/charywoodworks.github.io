Wood Polymer Composite (WPC) is a 100% waterproof and termite-proof engineered material made from wood flour and plastic polymers. Ideal for bathrooms, balconies, and areas with high humidity. Heavier than plywood but extremely durable in wet conditions. Does not swell, rot, or support fungal growth. Available in solid and foam variants with different density levels. Can be finished with PVC laminates or painted.
