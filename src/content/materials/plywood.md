Plywood is the most versatile and commonly used material for home furniture in India. Available in different grades — BWR (Boiling Water Resistant) for kitchens and bathrooms, and commercial grade for dry areas. It offers excellent strength-to-weight ratio, screw-holding capacity, and can be finished with laminates, veneers, or paint. Branded plywood from manufacturers like Century, Greenply, and Kitply ensures consistent quality.
