Natural wood veneers are thin slices of real wood applied over plywood or MDF substrates. They offer the beauty and warmth of solid wood at a fraction of the cost and weight. Each veneer sheet has unique grain patterns, making every piece of furniture one-of-a-kind. Available in species like teak, walnut, oak, ash, and rosewood. Finished with clear polyurethane to protect the surface while showcasing the natural wood character. Ideal for those who love the solid wood look but want lighter, more affordable furniture.
