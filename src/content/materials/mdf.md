Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) is an engineered wood product made from compressed wood fibers. Its smooth, uniform surface makes it ideal for painted finishes, CNC-routed patterns, and profile edges. More affordable than plywood for non-structural applications. Not suitable for moisture-prone areas unless using the HMR (High Moisture Resistant) variant. Perfect for decorative furniture, wall paneling, and display shelves.
