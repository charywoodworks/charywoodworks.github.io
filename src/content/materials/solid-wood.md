Solid wood (teak, sheesham, sal) is the most durable and prestigious material for furniture. It offers natural beauty with unique grain patterns, ages gracefully, and can be refinished multiple times. Best suited for furniture pieces meant to last a lifetime — dining tables, bed frames, doors, and pooja mandirs. Requires proper seasoning to prevent warping in Hyderabad's climate.
